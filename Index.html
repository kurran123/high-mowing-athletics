Athletics — High Mowing School

At High Mowing School, our Recreational Athletics Program recognizes that sports are an integral, fun part of adolescent life. Our approach fits within a broader movement to reclaim youth sports for what they should have always been: fun, communal, and participatory.

Overview

Playing in leagues and games across the region, our Athletics program in the 2025-26 school year is made up of eight teams, across five sports, including soccer, cross country, basketball, volleyball, and ultimate frisbee.

Currently, about 70% of our students participate in our Recreational Athletics Programs, Competitive Soccer Programs, and Circus Studies Program. There are no tryouts or cuts — everyone plays on our teams. Each athlete is inspired to reach their highest potential, contributing however they best can.

Philosophy

The Athletics Program at High Mowing School is an integral component of the middle school and high school educational experience. Athletics aims to support physical, emotional, and social development of each student-athlete. Through athletics, students develop and expand a sense of fairness, will, discipline, grit, resilience, responsibility, collaboration, and respect for others.
